#### WE ARE INVITING YOU TO THE WORLD OF GENUINE LINEN

Our linen is naturally grown and made from the flax plant in Eastern Europe. We use finest technology in weaving and eco-friendly fabric production, taking extra care in ensuring the preservation of all of linen's extraordinary benefits.

Did you know that **linen** is the strongest natural fiber in the world?

* _Linen fabric is stronger than cotton. That means your household linen will last you for years, and will be able to withstand a lot of washings without losing its qualities._

or Did you know that **linen**is a highly breathable material and also is a great heat conductor?

* _Its air permeable tests show very high results. Linen fibers are hollow and move air and moisture naturally, much better than silk and cotton. As well linen will keep you warm in winter and cool during summer time. _

Also **linen** is known as a health beneficial fabric

* _It helps reduce stress for a better nights sleep, massages your body and exfoliates skin. It can also help to repel micro organisms, in other words preventing bacterial growth, which is very important for home textile such as towels and bedsheets. Linen is non-allergenic._
